My choice

I chose my topic to be my favorite scriptures because they provide guidance through revelation and teach valuable lessons from the experiences of ancient prophets. As I study these sacred records, I learn to trust in Jesus Christ, strengthen my faith, and seek direction from the Holy Ghost in daily life.

In addition to the scriptures, I am grateful for modern-day prophets who continue to receive revelation from God. Their messages in General Conference and other devotionals help me understand gospel principles, make righteous decisions, and remain steadfast on the covenant path as I navigate life's challenges and opportunities.
